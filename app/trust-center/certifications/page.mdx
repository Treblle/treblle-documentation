# Certifications

Treblle is a software company that has implemented policies and procedures to keep user and company data safe. We have a rigorous assessment process to ensure infrastructure, sub-processors, third-party vendors, and employees have strict security policies in place.

We also have a [detailed privacy policy](https://treblle.com/privacy-policy).

While no environment can guarantee absolute security, Treblle makes it easy to [mask sensitive data](/explore-treblle/platform/data-masking/), ensure [API security](/explore-treblle/platform/api-security/dashboard), as well as scan for vulnerabilities with our compliance feature.

For more information, please visit our [Security page](https://treblle.com/security).
